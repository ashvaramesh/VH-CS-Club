Projects: ImpactLink

A community-driven platform connecting high school and college students with impactful volunteering, internships, and civic engagement opportunities.

Overview

ImpactLink originated from the recognition that students often struggle to find meaningful community involvement that aligns with their interests and aspirations. Initially, we surveyed local high school and college clubs to identify common pain points: scattered opportunity listings, unclear time commitments, and limited ability to measure personal impact.

Early prototypes focused on simple opportunity lists, but user testing revealed a need for personalization. We introduced badges, leaderboards, and progress graphs to gamify engagement, spurring healthy competition and a sense of achievement. Over multiple design iterations, we integrated real-time filters by category and location, ensuring users could quickly find opportunities aligned with their civic interests. Accessibility considerations, such as screen reader compatibility and dark mode, were prioritized from the start to create an inclusive environment. The journey has been one of continuous refinement: integrating Firebase for real-time data, collecting feedback from mentors and peers, and evolving the UI into a streamlined, user-friendly experience.

Key Benefits

  • Track volunteer hours with intuitive progress graphs
  • Earn gamified badges & compete on dynamic leaderboards
  • Customize profiles with personal skills, goals, and unique avatars
  • Discover opportunities instantly via category & location filters
  • Benefit from accessibility features, including screen reader compatibility and dark mode
